Accessing Sent Emails : [Updated as of May-2024]

Access a Record of Past Communications to Stay Informed About Important Updates, Instructions, and Correspondence.


To access a record of past communications and stay informed about important updates, instructions, and correspondence, follow these steps:


1. Navigate to the dashboard: Log in to the HRMS and access the main dashboard.
2. Locate the "Quick Access" button: Look for a prominently displayed button or menu option labeled "Quick Access" on the dashboard.
3. Click on "Mail Log" from the dropdown: Upon clicking the "Quick Access" button, a dropdown menu will appear. Select "Mail Log" from this menu.
4. Review the mail logs: Once you've selected "Mail Log," a list of sent emails or communication logs will be displayed, providing access to past correspondence.
5. Utilize search and filter options: Use any search or filter functionalities provided to narrow down the list based on specific criteria such as date, recipient, or subject.
6. Click on individual emails for details: To view the contents of a specific email, click on its corresponding entry in the mail log.
7. Take necessary actions: Based on the information retrieved, stay informed about important updates, instructions, and correspondence, and proceed with any required follow-up actions.


By following these steps, you can efficiently access and review sent emails to stay informed about past communications within the HRMS platform.
